Welcome to the Ministry of Magic's Pay Guide!

Galleons are the pay currency used in the Wizarding World of Harry Potter! Here at the Ministry, you can earn Galleons by completing various tasks.

2 (TWO) Galleons are equivalent to 1 (ONE) Habbo Credit or 1 (ONE) Galleon is equivalent to 1 (ONE) Habbo Credit if they are used to buy a rank.

To be able to earn Galleons, you will need to:

1. Be a member of the Ministry.

2. Have a verified account on MoM Portal.

3. Complete tasks and post under the #galleons-vouchers channel.

Pay Rules

1. The station priority rule is to be followed at ALL times.

2. Only 5iC+ members (excluding S.P.E.W. members) and Pay Team members are eligible to vouch for another member.

3. Galleons are non-transferable to another member.

4. Galleons are reset at the end of every month.

5. Ministry members are allowed to claim 100 (ONE HUNDRED) Galleons per day. There will be no limit for Galleon conversion to ranks.

6. Members can convert their Galleons for a rank purchase at a rate of 1 (ONE) Galleon to 1 (ONE) Habbo Credits. This will be logged as a rank sale.

7. Members filling stations for more than 1 (ONE) hour, are to post a vouch at the end of every hour.

8. Members are to ensure that their own vouch is correct and reacted by their voucher (if applicable). If there are no reactions from your voucher it will be deemed as invalid.

9. Pay can be claimed anytime. If your request has not been processed after 24 hours, you can tag @Pay Team in #Miscellaneous-requests if you have reached the minimum requirements. (Please do not tag if you have not waited 24 hours after you claimed on portal)

10. When members leave the Ministry, any unclaimed Galleons in the portal will be removed.

11. When a member is bopped/teleported to FTB, or leaves to FTB/AFK seats, their hour resets.
